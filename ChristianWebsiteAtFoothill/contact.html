Welcome

Christians on Campus at Foothill College warmly invites you to join our christian community and church family. We are a group of college students from diverse backgrounds who come together as loving seekers of Christ in His Word. Whether through on-campus Bible studies or fellowship with other Christians in the church community, our desire is to grow in Christ together "with all those who call on the Lord out of a pure heart" (2 Timothy 2:22).
